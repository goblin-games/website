First person simulator of throwing knives with realistic physics and destruction

Knife Throwing Simulator is the game about knives and how to throw them! Take a knife and throw it wherever you want! Different targets and game modes to choose from.

Build your knife! Take materials and craft new deadly blades by your choice! Put the handle you like and you ready to destroy! Make your weapon even more destructive with powerfull runes and turn your blade into burning fireball or flying lightning!

Key Features:

  • Real 3D first person expirience — nice graphics with reasonable limits for smooth gameplay
  • Simple controls — play comfortable even if you have just one arm with 3 fingers as your character in game
  • Physics and Destruction! — control force and torque of the throw with one motion as in real world. Everything else depends on the laws of physics
  • Different game modes — several modes to choose from, including darts or items knockdown
